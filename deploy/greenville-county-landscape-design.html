Landscape Design Across Greenville County, SC

Great outdoor spaces don't happen by accident — they start with a thoughtful design. Redwood Landscaping LLC brings professional landscape design to homeowners throughout Greenville County, from Travelers Rest to Simpsonville, from the Augusta Road corridor to the growing communities around Five Forks and beyond. If you can imagine it, we can design it and build it.

Greenville County is one of the fastest-growing regions in the Southeast, and with growth comes a demand for outdoor spaces that match the quality of the homes being built and renovated. Whether you're in a historic neighborhood near downtown Greenville or on acreage in the northern part of the county, our design process respects your property's natural features while creating the outdoor living experience you want.

Our Greenville County Design Process

Every landscape design begins with a conversation — on your property, walking the space together, talking about how you want to use it. Do you need a patio for entertaining? A fire pit area for fall evenings? Privacy screening from a busy road? A play space for kids that still looks polished? We listen first, sketch second. Our hand-drawn design concepts give you a clear picture of the final result before construction begins.

Our designs integrate hardscape and softscape into a unified plan. Stone patios and walkways define outdoor rooms and traffic patterns. Retaining walls terrace slopes into usable level spaces — a common need in Greenville County's rolling terrain. Plant selection is guided by what thrives in Zone 7b/8a: Southern magnolias, crepe myrtles, hydrangeas, azaleas, ornamental grasses, and native perennials that provide four-season interest with manageable maintenance.

For clients who want to take it further, we design outdoor kitchens with built-in grills, prep counters, and bar seating. Water features — from simple bubbling urns to recirculating streams — add sound and movement. Landscape lighting extends usability into the evening and highlights specimen trees and architectural features after dark. Every element is drawn to scale, with materials and plant species specified so there are no surprises.

Once the design is approved, our crew executes the plan with precision. We manage the entire build — demolition, grading, hardscape installation, irrigation rough-in, planting, sod, mulch, and final cleanup. One team, one point of contact, one beautiful result. And because we're local to Greenville County, we're here for follow-up care, seasonal refreshes, and whatever your landscape needs as it matures.
