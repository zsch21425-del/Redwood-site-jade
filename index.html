FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve Slater, Marietta, Greenville, Travelers Rest, Simpsonville, Five Forks, Tigerville, Cleveland, Greer, Easley — and throughout Upstate South Carolina. If you're not sure if we cover your area, just call and ask.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely. Every project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate. We come to your property, assess what's needed, and give you an honest price. No pressure, no sales pitch — just straight answers.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Redwood Landscaping LLC is fully licensed and insured for all services we provide. Your property and your peace of mind are protected.

What's the difference between you and a big franchise?

We're local — we live in Slater-Marietta. We know Upstate SC soil, climate, and what actually works here. You talk directly to the owner, not a call center. And we do full-service design and build — not just chemical lawn treatments.

How soon can you start?

Most lawn maintenance and small projects can start within 1–2 weeks. Larger landscape designs depend on scope and season. Call us at (864) 357-4530 for current availability.

Do you work with HOAs or commercial properties?

Yes — we work with homeowners associations, commercial properties, and residential clients throughout the Upstate. We understand HOA requirements and can work within your community's guidelines.
