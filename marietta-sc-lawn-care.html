Lawn Care Services in Marietta, SC

A thick, green, healthy lawn transforms a house into a home — and in Marietta, South Carolina, the growing season demands consistent, knowledgeable care. Redwood Landscaping LLC provides professional lawn care services to Marietta residents who want their grass to be the envy of the neighborhood without spending every weekend behind a mower.

Marietta's warm summers and mild winters mean your lawn is growing — or recovering — nearly year-round. Our lawn care program is built specifically for Upstate South Carolina conditions: Bermuda and Fescue varieties that dominate local yards, the red clay soil that needs proper amendment, and the weed pressure that comes with humidity and rain. We don't apply a one-size-fits-all formula. Every Marietta lawn gets a custom care plan based on grass type, shade conditions, soil health, and your goals.

Complete Marietta Lawn Care Program

Weekly mowing during peak season keeps your lawn at the optimal height for your grass variety — never scalped, never left shaggy. We use commercial-grade equipment with sharp blades that cut clean rather than tear, reducing disease risk and giving your lawn that crisp, striped look. Edging along driveways, walkways, and beds provides the finishing touch that separates a good cut from a great one.

Fertilization is timed to Marietta's growing calendar. Early spring pre-emergent prevents crabgrass before it germinates. Late spring feeding fuels green-up and root development. Summer applications are balanced to avoid burning during heat stress. Fall winterizer strengthens roots for dormancy and ensures a faster, fuller green-up when spring returns. Every application uses professional-grade products applied at the correct rate — no guesswork, no waste.

Weed control targets what's actually growing in your lawn. Broadleaf weeds like dandelion and clover, grassy invaders like crabgrass and nutsedge, and persistent problems like ground ivy all require different approaches. We identify, spot-treat, and follow up — because killing weeds once isn't enough if conditions let them return. Our integrated approach combines pre-emergent barriers with post-emergent treatment for season-long control.

Seasonal services round out the program: core aeration in fall relieves compaction in Marietta's heavy clay, overseeding thickens thin spots, and lime applications correct the acidic soil pH that holds back nutrient uptake. Leaf removal in autumn, spring cleanup in March — we handle the calendar so you don't have to.
