What We Build

Hardscaping Crafted to Last

Every hardscape project is built with quality materials, proper drainage, and the craftsmanship that comes from knowing Upstate SC soil and weather.

Flagstone Patios

Custom-designed flagstone patios that add beauty and function to your backyard. We use natural stone and precision installation for patios that look stunning for decades. Perfect for entertaining, dining, or simply relaxing outdoors.

Retaining Walls

Structural and decorative retaining walls that hold back soil, prevent erosion, and create usable terraced space. We build with concrete block, natural stone, or timber — engineered for drainage and long-term stability in Upstate clay soil.

Stone Walkways

Beautiful stone pathways that guide visitors through your landscape. We design and install flagstone, paver, or gravel walkways with proper base preparation, edging, and drainage. Curved or straight — designed to complement your property.

Fire Pits

Custom fire pits that become the centerpiece of your outdoor living space. Built with fire-rated stone or block, surrounded by seating areas. Gas or wood-burning options available. Extend your outdoor season into the cooler evenings.

Our Process

From Concept to Completion

1

Design

We visit your property, discuss your vision, take measurements, and create a hardscape plan that fits your space, budget, and lifestyle.

2

Build

Our experienced crew handles excavation, base preparation, and installation with precision. We respect your property, keep the site clean, and communicate throughout.

3

Finish

Final grading, joint sand, cleanup, and a walk-through with you to ensure everything exceeds expectations. We don't leave until you love it.
